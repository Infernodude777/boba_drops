Into the Void

Our game is about a man who corrects all of his wrong doings.

He starts his adventure by waking up in a graveyard. He has to fight off many spirits to survive. Little does he know, those are the past versions of himself doing bad things.

As he fights off the spirits, he picks up many items to aid him in battle.

Once he beats all of the spirits, he reaches the afterlife, just like how in ancient Egyptian mythology, one must fight off spirits to reach the afterlife.
